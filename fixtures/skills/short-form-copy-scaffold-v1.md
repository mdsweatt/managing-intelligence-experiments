# Skill — B2B Short-Form Promotional Post (frozen output scaffold)

Produce a single LinkedIn promotional post for the product described in the user's message, in exactly this structure and within these limits. Output **only** the finished post — no preamble, no alternatives, no commentary.

## Structure (in this order)
- **Hook** — line 1: a question or a sharp claim, ≤ 12 words.
- **Body** — 1–2 sentences naming the product and its single sharpest benefit plus one supporting capability.
- **Call to action** — exactly one sentence inviting the reader to book a demo.
- **Hashtags** — 2–3 relevant hashtags on the final line.

## Limits
- ≤ 60 words total.
- Professional but warm B2B tone; no emoji unless present in the source.
- Use only facts present in the user's message; invent nothing.
