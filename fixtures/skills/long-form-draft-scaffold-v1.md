# Skill — Leadership Decision Memo (frozen output scaffold)

Produce a single decision memo from the brief in the user's message, in exactly this structure and within these limits. Output **only** the memo, starting with the recommendation — no title page, no preamble, no commentary.

## Structure (in this order)
1. **Recommendation** — one line, up front, explicit: renew the current vendor **or** pilot a replacement.
2. **Background** — ≤ 120 words: the situation and why a decision is needed now.
3. **Options Considered** — renew vs. pilot, 2–4 sentences each, even-handed.
4. **Cost & Budget Implications** — tie to the brief's stated budget cap; state no invented figures.
5. **Risks** — 3–5 bullets, each a risk paired with a mitigation.
6. **Next Steps** — a numbered timeline that fits the brief's decision window.

## Limits
- ~800 words total (±10%).
- Professional business prose.
- Use only facts, names, and figures present in the brief; invent none.
