Meridian Foods is evaluating whether to bring its outsourced customer-support operation back in-house over the next eighteen months. The current vendor handles roughly 40,000 contacts a month across phone, email, and chat, at a blended cost of 6.10 dollars per contact. Customer satisfaction has plateaued at 78% and escalations are rising. An in-house build would require hiring, tooling, and training investment, but promises tighter product feedback loops. Leadership wants a report assessing costs, risks, staffing, timeline, and a recommended path before the annual budget is locked.
