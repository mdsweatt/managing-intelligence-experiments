Northwind Logistics' regional dispatch team has missed its on-time delivery target for three consecutive quarters, slipping from 94% to 88%. Root causes cited internally: an aging routing tool, driver shortages in two metros, and warehouse handoff delays. Leadership wants a recommendation on whether to renew the current routing vendor or pilot a replacement. Budget for any pilot is capped at 150,000 dollars for the fiscal year. A decision is expected before the next quarterly planning cycle in eight weeks.
